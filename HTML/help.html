How can I book books ?

  1. Select from Home "Books"
  2. Press the "Continue" button
  3. Books will be shown to you , select the books you want
  4. Fill out the form by name , number , e-mail or number an e-mail togather , and then press the "submit" button
  5. the following information will be shown you :
    • books that have already registered " if you have booked before "
    • the books you now have booked
    • total price and Id book
    • your information " name , number , e-mail "
  6. If all your information is correct, click on the "Confirm" button

How can I delete my registration?

  1. press the " To delete your registration " in Home
  2. Enter your ID "Customized for the course you want to delete you"
  3. After pressing the "Submit" button, your information will appear to you
    In case that you have an error in your information, you can simply correct the ID and press the "Submit" button again.
  4. If all your information is correct, click on the "Confirm" button

How can I Edit my registration?

  1. press the " Edit your registration " in Home
  2. Enter your ID "Customized for the course you want to edit"
  3. fill in only the fields you want to modify
  4. After pressing the "Submit" button, your information will appear to you
    In case that you have an error in your old information, you can simply correct the ID and press the "Submit" button again.
    In case that you have an error in your new information that you entered, you can simply correct the error and press the "Submit" button again.
  5. If all your information is correct, click on the "Confirm" button
    6. When you change the subject , the book will change automatically "if you have registered on a book on the course registation page"

What if I forget the ID ?

  1. On the Edit an Delete page, there is "Restor your information" button , click on it
    • If you have registered by phone number, enter your phone number only (Do not enter the email if you have not entered it before)
    • If you have registered by email, enter your email only (Do not enter the phone number if you have not entered it before)
    • If you have registered by phone number and email, enter your phone number and email togather (Do not just enter one of them)
  3. Press "Submit" button
    4. If you do not comply with the steps above, your information will not appear correctly
